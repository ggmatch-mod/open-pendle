::: warning Experimental — use at your own risk
Community pools are **permissionless and unreviewed** — anyone can create one, and interacting with them can lose you funds. OpenPendle checks that a market came from a Pendle factory it recognizes, but it **cannot vouch for the assets or SY contracts underneath**. Please read [Risks &amp; disclosures](/risks) before you transact.
:::
